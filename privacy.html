Privacy Policy

Profile Frame Maker respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

How the extension works

  • All images (profile pictures and frames) are processed locally in your browser.
  • No image or personal data is uploaded to any server.
  • The extension does not access your online accounts or other personal information.

Permissions

The extension only uses the storage permission to save optional settings locally. It does not access any external websites or your private data.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in this file on GitHub.
